Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Our standard admission prices are as follows:

  • Adults (18+): $15
  • Children (3-17): $10
  • Seniors (65+): $12
  • Children under 3: Free

We also offer family passes and annual memberships for frequent visitors. Special exhibits or activities may have additional fees.

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking in advance, especially during weekends, holidays, and school breaks when we tend to be busier. Advanced booking guarantees your entry at your preferred time and helps us manage visitor flow.

School groups and birthday parties must be booked at least two weeks in advance to ensure availability.

Yes, we have a free parking lot for visitors with approximately 100 spaces. During peak times, overflow parking is available at the adjacent community center lot. We also have designated spaces for buses and school groups.

We have a café on-site that serves a variety of sandwiches, salads, snacks, and beverages. We also have a picnic area outside where you can enjoy your own packed lunches. Please note that food and drinks are not allowed in the exhibit areas.

Yes, Apollo is fully accessible for visitors with disabilities. We have ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms throughout the facility. We also offer sensory-friendly sessions for visitors with sensory sensitivities on the first Tuesday of each month. Please contact us if you have specific accessibility requirements.
