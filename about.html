About Us

Discover what makes Apollo a special place for children's education

Our Story

Apollo was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate educators and scientists who believed that learning should be an exciting adventure for children. What began as a small exhibition space has grown into a comprehensive educational center visited by thousands of children and families each year.

Our mission is to inspire curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning through interactive exhibits, hands-on activities, and engaging educational programs. We believe that every child has the potential to be a scientist, explorer, artist, or innovator, and we're dedicated to nurturing those possibilities.

Today, Apollo continues to grow and evolve, constantly adding new exhibits and programs while staying true to our founding vision of making learning fun and accessible for all.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Apollo

Curiosity

We foster a spirit of inquiry and wonder in children, encouraging them to ask questions and seek answers. Curiosity is the foundation of learning and discovery.

Inclusivity

We create a welcoming environment for children of all backgrounds, abilities, and learning styles. Education should be accessible to everyone.

Excellence

We strive for the highest quality in our exhibits, programs, and educational content, constantly updating and improving what we offer.

Creativity

We believe that innovation and imagination are essential skills for the future, and we encourage creative thinking and expression in all our activities.

Environmental Stewardship

We teach children about the importance of caring for our planet and making sustainable choices for a better future for all living things.
